Settlements

In Settlements, you can process the purchase orders and invoices raised. This screen can be accessed through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Purchase Ledger > Accounts Payable > Settlements from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Purchase Ledger > Accounts Payable > Settlements.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    •  Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Accounts Payable > Settlements from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Purchase Ledger > Accounts Payable > Settlements.

The screen comprises three folders:

  • Invoices - Search and select an invoice, record a payment, write off or cancel an invoice.

  • Purchase Orders - Search and select a purchase order, record a payment, write off or cancel a purchase order, or post the purchase order.

  • Transactions - Search and select a receipt or payment, or cancel a transaction.

Note: It is possible to set the authority level for the Print button seen in Invoice, Purchase Orders and Transactions folders to control which users can print. This is to be done in Configuration > Settings > Customise > Field Settings.
