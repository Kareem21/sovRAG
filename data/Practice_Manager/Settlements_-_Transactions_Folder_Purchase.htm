Settlements - Transactions Folder

Receipts and payments are displayed here. The screen is divided into three parts. The first part is where you enter your search criteria. The transactions that match the criteria are shown in the table in the middle part of the screen together with function buttons that are related to them. Invoices and purchase orders related to the transaction can be seen by selecting the appropriate tab in the bottom part of the screen.

 

Search and Summary

Enter your search criteria here. The Summary box on the right of the search fields provides a snapshot of the matter balances, outstanding invoices and outstanding drafts for the selected Supplier File.

  • Filter - The list of items displayed defaults to the selected Supplier File, but you may also choose to display information for the Revenue Entity.

  • Receipts/Payments - Tick the checkboxes that correspond to the information you want to display.

  • Search/Refresh - Click this to refresh the list displayed in the Transaction table.

 

Transaction list

The transactions that match the search criteria are shown here.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Cancel - Click this to cancel the selected receipt or payment.

  • Print - Click to print the receipt.

  • View Journal - Click to display the journal details.

 

For the selected invoice, you can select the tabs below to view additional information:

  • Transactions - This tab does not display information for the Transactions folder.

  • Lines (Details) - This tab will display the line information for the selected transaction.
