Settlements - Matters Folder

This folder shows Matter balance information for the selected file, and this is where you enter Matter receipts and payments. The screen is divided into three parts. The first part is where you enter your search criteria. The Matters that match the criteria are shown in the table in the middle part of the screen together with command buttons that are related to Matters. Action and Transaction related to the matter can be seen by selecting the appropriate tab at the bottom part of the screen.

 

Search and Summary

Enter your search criteria here. The Summary box on the right of the search fields provides a snapshot of the matter balances, outstanding invoices and outstanding drafts for the selected Billing File.

  • Filter - In Viewpoint - Home screen, the list of items displayed defaults to the selected Billing File, but you may choose to display information based on the Billing Address, Client or Revenue Entity. In File Maintenance screen, this is fixed to the selected Billing file.

  • Balance<>0, Active and Closed - Tick the checkboxes that correspond to the information you want to display.

  • Search / Refresh - Click to display search results or refresh the list displayed in the Matter table.

 

Matter list

The Matters that match the search criteria are shown here.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Receipt - Click this to record a receipt of funds to the Matter, such as when you receive an advance from the client.

    1. Enter the details for the matter receipt:

      • Billing File - This field is inactive and defaults to the chosen Billing File.

      • Matter - This field is inactive and defaults to the chosen Matter.

      • Currency - This field is inactive and defaults to the Matter currency.

      • Amount Rec'd (DR) - Enter the amount received.

      • Received from - Enter the name of the payee. This field defaults to the chosen Billing File name.

      • Ref/cheque# - Enter the reference or cheque number of the receipt. This field is mandatory.

      • Bank Account - Enter the bank account the payment is to be credited to. The bank accounts listed here are those related to the Matter's Revenue Company.

      • (Bank) Charges - Enter any charges that may be incurred.

      • Receipt Date - Enter the date of the receipt. You can set the default date in Viewpoint - Home > Configuration > Settings > Customise > Field Settings where Menu Group = Home [1], Section = Practice Management [GPRACMGR] and Screen = Receivables [GPSARECR] to either:

        • Today - Enter NOW in Default

        • Last entered date - Enter LASTDATE in Default

      • Exchange to <base currency> - Defaults to the exchange rate set up in the Exchange Rates Master. This number can be overwritten if the Matter and base currencies differ.

      • Note - Enter receipt notes.

      • Print Voucher - Tick to print a voucher.

      • Charges - Click to create a disbursement.

    2. Click Update.

     

  • Payment - Click this to make a payment from the Matter balance.

    1. Enter the details for the matter payment:

      • Billing File - This field is inactive and defaults to the chosen Billing File.

      • Matter - This field is inactive and defaults to the chosen Matter.

      • Currency - This field is inactive and defaults to the Matter currency.

      • Amount Paid (CR) - Enter the amount received.

      • Paid to - Enter the name of the recipient. This field defaults to the chosen Billing File name.

      • Ref/cheque# - Enter the reference or cheque number of the receipt. This field is mandatory.

      • Bank Account - Enter the bank account the payment is to be credited to. The bank accounts listed here are those related to the Matter's Revenue Company.

      • (Bank) Charges - Enter any charges that may be incurred.

      • Payment Date - Enter the date of the payment. You can set the default date in Viewpoint - Home > Configuration > Settings > Customise > Field Settings where Menu Group = Home [1], Section = Practice Management [GPRACMGR] and Screen = Receivables [GPSARECR]  to either:

        • Today - Enter NOW in Default

        • Last entered date - Enter LASTDATE in Default

      • Exchange to <base currency> - Defaults to the exchange rate set up in the Exchange Rates Master. This number can be overwritten if the Matter and base currencies differ.

      • Note - Enter payment notes.

      • Print Voucher - Tick to print a voucher.

      • Charges - Click to create a disbursement.

    1. Click Update.

     

  • Revalue - Click this to amend the exchange rate of the Matter currency to base currency. A receipt will be issued with 0 amount for the exchange difference.

    1. Enter the new exchange rate.

    1. Click Ok to update.

     

  • Print - Click this to print the Matter statement. It will be sent to the Document Queue.

 

For the selected Matter, you can select the tabs below to view additional information:

  • Actions - This tab shows the actions for the Matter, if any. You can also add new action. Refer to Action Management.

  • Transactions - This tab shows the receipt/payment transactions related to the Matter.
