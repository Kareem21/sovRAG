Registered Office

You can charge your clients for the use of specified addresses as the registered office address of their entities in Viewpoint Entity Administration.

 

Address list

This shows all the addresses that have been set up as a registered office charge.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click to add a new registered office.

    1. Enter the details:

      • Address Card - Select the address.

      • Service - Select the service to be added to the Billing File's Recurring Fees.

    2. Click Update.

  • Edit - Click to edit the details of the registered office charge.

  • Delete - Click to delete a registered office charge.
