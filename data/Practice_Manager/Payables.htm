Payables

The Payables folder provides you with the ability to make payments for purchase orders. This screen can be accessed through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Purchase Ledger > Accounts Payable > Payables from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Purchase Ledger > Accounts Payable > Payables.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Accounts Payable > Payables from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.  

    • In Section Menu, click File > Purchase Ledger > Accounts Payable > Payables.

You can click on the following to minimise or expand the sections in this screen:

  • - To minimise this section and hide the command buttons. In the grid, only the selected row will be seen.

  • - To expand the section and show the command buttons. If the subtotal line is enabled, it will only be displayed in the expanded view.

 

Select button

In Viewpoint - Home, there are two Select buttons:

  • The first one allows you to select a different administrator, Administrator Group or User Group.

    1. Click Select.

    2. In the Selection Setup, select your option from:

      • Favourites - Click the desired option.

      • Users - Tick to select the users.

      • Administrator Group - Tick to select the Administrator Groups.

      • User Groups - Tick to select the User Groups.

    3. Tick Apply to all screens if you want your selection to be applied to all other screens in Viewpoint - Home screen.

    4. Click Select.

     

  • The second one is to enter additional search criteria to filter the list.

    After you have entered the criteria, click Apply.

 

Invoices list

This shows purchase invoices that have been posted. To select multiple invoices, tick the checkbox for the invoice.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Settlement - Click this to enter payment for the selected invoice.

    1. In the Payment on Cr/Dr Note screen, enter the payment details.

    2. Click Update when done.

  • Settle Multiple - Click this to settle multiple selected invoices in full. This will still create individual settlement transaction.

    1. Tick the invoices you want to settle.

    2. Click Settle Multiple.

    3. Enter the settlement details such as the bank account and note.

    4. Click Update.

  • Print Statement - Click this to print statement for the selected Supplier File.

  • Reprint Invoice - Click this to reprint the selected invoice.

  • Block - Click this to block payment for the selected invoice. This feature only functions if you have selected 'Batch/Approval of AP payment' in the Practice Management settings.

    1. In the Block Payment screen, enter the reason for the block in Note field.

    2. Click Update.

  • Unblock - Click this to remove payment block on the selected invoice.

  • View Journal - Click to display the journal details.
