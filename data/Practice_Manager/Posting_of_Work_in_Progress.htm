Posting of WIP (Work in Progress)

The Posting of WIP enables you to pick up recurring fees that are due and post these to a draft invoice. It can also retrieve time charges and disbursements that were entered and add them to the same invoice as the recurring fees.

You can access this through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Sales Ledger Ribbon button and select Sales Ledger > Work in Progress > Posting of WIP from the navigation listing in Sales Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Sales Ledger > Work in Progress > Posting of WIP.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Sales Ledger Ribbon button and select Work in Progress > Posting of WIP from the navigation listing in Sales Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Sales Ledger > Work in Progress > Posting of WIP.

The screen is divided into:

  • Select

  • Proposed Drafts
