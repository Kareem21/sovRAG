Settlements - Invoice Folder

Posted invoices are listed here and you can filter the table to show outstanding invoices, those that were overpaid and those that have been settled in full. The screen is divided into three parts. The first part is where you enter your search criteria. The invoices that match the criteria are shown in the table in the middle part of the screen together with function buttons that are related to the invoices. Action, Transaction, Matter and detail lines related to the invoice can be seen by selecting the appropriate tab in the bottom part of the screen.

 

Search and Summary

Enter your search criteria here. The Summary box on the right of the search fields provides a snapshot of the matter balances, outstanding invoices and outstanding drafts for the selected Billing File.

  • Filter - In Viewpoint - Home screen, the list of items displayed defaults to the selected Billing File, but you may choose to display information based on the Billing Address, Client or Revenue Entity. In File Maintenance screen, this is fixed to the selected Billing file.

  • Outstanding, Overpaid and Settled - Tick the checkboxes that correspond to the information you want to display.

  • Invoice No., Draft No. and Reference - Select any of these options and enter the invoice number, draft number or reference you want to look for. For multiple values, separate with a comma.

  • Search/Refresh - Click to refresh the list displayed in the invoice table.

 

Invoice list

The invoices that match the search criteria are shown here.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Receipt/Payment - Click this to enter receipt for the selected invoice. The Receipt on Dr/Cr Note screen will open in a separate window for you to enter the receipt details. Refer to Settlements - Multiple Documents and Invoice Lines.

    1. In the Receipt on Dr/Cr Note screen, enter the receipt details.

    2. Click Update.

  • Settle to Matter - Click this to settle the invoice against a cash advance.

  • Invoice - This button is inactive in this folder.

  • Cancel D/C Note - Click this to cancel the selected outstanding invoice by posting a credit/debit note using the Credit Note Wizard. It is available for invoices where the invoice's Matter status is set to Open [0], Blocked Time Entry [2] or On Hold [3] and where the type is NOT Credit Note - Cancel [CR_C]. Refer to  Settlements - Invoice Cancellation.

  • Notes - Click this to add notes for the that particular invoice.

    You can record the settlement and bad debt details in the Notes screen. The information entered here can be picked up in Accounting for the Posting Rule function.

     

    Invoice Type

    This shows the invoice type and is non-editable by default. To allow a user to change the type, change the field authority accordingly.

     

    Note (general)

    Enter general details for the invoice including notes about the bad debt.

     

    Settlement Status

    Update the settlement status of the invoice. The drop-down values are being maintained in the Invoice Settlement Status [INSS] lookup table.

     

    Status Due Date

    Enter the date for the settlement status.

     

    Provision in <currency>

    Enter the amount of the provision made in the billing currency.

     

    Exchange

    The exchange rate from the Matter currency to the base currency will be displayed here. The rates are maintained in the Exchange Rate Master in Home > Practice Management.

     

    Provision in <base currency>

    Enter the amount of the provision after conversion to the base currency.

     

    Provision Note

    Enter notes on the provision made.

     

    Update

    Click this to save the information.

  • Fee Payable - Click this to find out if there is any fee payable in the selected invoice. The information will be displayed in a separate window. If there is no fee payable, there will not be any information displayed in the Fee Details and Penalty sections of the screen.

  • Invoice Penalty - Click this to invoice penalties caused by the late payment of government fees.

  • View Journal - Click to display the journal details.

 

For the selected invoice, you can select the tabs below to view additional information:

  • Actions - This tab shows the actions for the invoice, if any. You can also add new action.

  • Transactions - This tab shows the receipt/payment transactions related to the invoice.

  • Matters - This tab shows the active Matters of the Billing File.

  • Lines (Details) - The individual detail lines of the selected invoice are displayed here. Double click a line to see the line information, such as the VAT %, exchange rate and discount given.

  • Line Source - This tab is seen only if the selected invoice contains invoice lines from Time or Disbursement entries.

  • Time Entries - This tab is seen if the selected invoice contains invoice line from Time entries. Click this to see Time entries for the invoice including the non-chargeable ones.

  • Disbursement Entries - This tab is seen if the selected invoice contains invoice line from Disbursement entries. Click here to see Disbursement entries for the invoice including the non-chargeable ones.
