Settle Debit/Credit Note to Matter Account

Invoices can be settled directly with a receipt from the client, or it can be offset against advance payments already made by the client for a particular Matter. The Settle Debit/Credit note to Matter Account screen is where you enter the amount you want to settle to a Matter account. There should be some balance in the Matter account before this is done.
