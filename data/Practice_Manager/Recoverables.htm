Recoverables

The Recoverables screen lists all items that can be or have been recovered from Billing Files. This screen can be accessed through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Purchase Ledger > Recoverables from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Purchase Ledger > Recoverables.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Invoicing > Recoverables from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Purchase Ledger > Invoicing > Recoverables.

 

Filters

You can filter the list by:

  • Service - Select the service.

  • Created -from and -to - Enter the date range you want to search through.

  • Select Fees - Select whether to show processed fees, unprocessed fees or both.

  • Invoice/Draft - Select whether to search through posted invoices or purchase orders.

 

Refresh

Click this to refresh the list after you have made adjustments to the filters.

 

Purchase order list

This lists all the purchase orders that match your selection criteria.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Recover - Click to open the Recover screen.

  • Select All - Click to select all records listed in the table.

  • Deselect All - Click to deselect the selected records.

  • 'Processed' - Click to mark the selected record(s) as recovered. Marking an item as recovered does not have any bearing on the system.

  • 'Unprocess' - Click to mark the selected record(s) as not recovered.
