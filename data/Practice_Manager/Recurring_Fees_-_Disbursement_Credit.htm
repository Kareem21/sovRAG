Recurring Fees - Disbursement Credit

Disbursement Credit Service refers to a type of recurring fee that allows for a specified amount of chargeable disbursement to not be invoiced for a Billing File or Matter. This is done using the service class 'Credit: Disbursement Charge [CD] with the credit specified through the setup of the service in the Recurring Fees screen as a recurring fee where:

  • The 'Fee Period - From' and 'Fee Period - To' determine the from and to date of the applicable credit period.

  • The credit is entered as a negative amount in Unit Price and it is to be charged in Advanced.

The credit will then be applied against chargeable disbursement entries with dates that fall in the applicable period. On Posting of WIP (where such fee exists and is due for invoicing), the program will calculate the chargeable amount for all disbursement entries due in the applicable period.

 

When the total chargeable amount is more or equal to the credit amount, the program will:

  • Add the Disbursement Credit Service recurring fee to the draft invoice for the full (credit) fee amount.

  • Invoice all the disbursement charges.

The resulting invoice will have one credit line (the Disbursement Credit Service recurring fee) and a line for each distinct service code used for the disbursement entries.

 

When the total chargeable amount is less than the credit amount and the applicable period's 'to date' is earlier than the date set for Posting Control Settings - WIP Time's Date Open, the program will:

  • Add the Disbursement Credit Service recurring fee to the draft invoice for a (credit) fee amount equal to all disbursement charges.

  • Invoice all the disbursement charges.

This is to say that the Disbursement Credit Service recurring fee is invoiced as the end date falls before the WIP Time's Date Open. Hence no more disbursement entries can be entered in the applicable period.

The resulting invoice will have one credit line (the Disbursement Credit Service recurring fee) and a line for each distinct service code used with the disbursement entries. The total invoice amount will be 0 unless there are other recurring fee or disbursements due for invoicing.

 

Otherwise, the Disbursement Credit Service and time are not invoiced as the (credit) fee amount has not been reached and disbursement charges can still be added for the applicable period.

Note that any disbursement charges with a date before the applicable period will be invoiced as usual (the Disbursement Credit Service is not applied).
