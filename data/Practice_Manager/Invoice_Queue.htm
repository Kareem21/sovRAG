Invoice Queue

Invoice Queue screen is available to queue all the invoices from Posting of WIP screen. From here, you can select the invoices to manually process them. This screen can be accessed through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Sales Ledger Ribbon button and select Sales Ledger > Work in Progress > Invoice Queue from the navigation listing in Sales Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Sales Ledger > Work in Progress > Invoice Queue.

 

Select button

Enter additional search criteria to filter the list. This is seen in Viewpoint - Home.

After you have entered the criteria, click Apply.

 

Filter

The filter allows you to view invoices that have been completed or not.

 

Disbursements list

This shows the existing invoices based on the criteria that you have set.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • View Invoices - Click this to view invoices.

  • Select All - Click this to select all the invoices.

  • Deselect All - Click this to deselect all invoices.

  • Process - Click this to process the invoices.

  • Delete - Click this to delete invoices.

  • Send Invoice to Document Queue - Click this to resend an invoice to the Document Queue screen. This will only be enabled when the invoices have the Draft No.
