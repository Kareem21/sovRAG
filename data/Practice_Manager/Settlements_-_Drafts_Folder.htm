Settlements - Drafts Folder

Draft invoices can be settled before they have been posted, if you access them from this folder. You can filter the information to show outstanding draft invoices, those that were overpaid and those that have been settled. The screen is divided into three parts. The first part is where you enter your search criteria. The draft invoices that match the criteria are shown in the table in the middle part of the screen together with function buttons that are related to the drafts. Action, Transaction, Matter and detail lines related to the draft can be seen by selecting the appropriate tab in the bottom part of the screen. These drafts can be edited in the Create/Edit Invoice screen.

 

Search and Summary

Enter your search criteria here. The Summary box on the right of the search fields provides a snapshot of the matter balances, outstanding invoices and outstanding drafts for the selected Billing File.

  • Filter - In Viewpoint - Home screen, the list of items displayed defaults to the selected Billing File, but you may choose to display information based on the Billing Address, Client or Revenue Entity. In File Maintenance screen, this is fixed to the selected Billing file.

  • Outstanding, Overpaid and Settled - Tick the checkboxes that correspond to the information you want to display.

  • Invoice No. - This cannot be used here as the drafts have not been posted.

  • Invoice No., Draft No. and Reference - Select the Draft no. or Reference option and enter the draft number or reference you want to look for. For multiple values, separate with a comma.

  • Search/Refresh - Click to refresh the list displayed in the invoice table.

 

Invoice list

The draft invoices that match the search criteria are shown here.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Receipt/Payment - Click this to enter receipt for the selected invoice. The Receipt on Dr/Cr Note screen will open in a separate window for you to enter the receipt details. Refer to Settlements - Multiple Documents and Invoice Lines

    1. In the Receipt on Dr/Cr Note screen, enter the receipt details.

    2. Click Update.

  • Settle to Matter - Click this to settle the draft invoice against a cash advance.

  • Invoice - Click this to post the draft.

  • Cancel D/C Note - This button is inactive in this folder. To cancel a draft, you need to delete it.

  • Print - Click this to print the selected draft invoice. Once a draft has been printed, it will be locked for editing. You need to reopen the draft to make further changes.

    The options that are available depend on the document template:

      Deliver to the Document Queue

  • Notes - Click this to add notes for the that particular draft.

    You can record the settlement and bad debts details in the Notes screen. The information entered here can be picked up in Accounting for the Posting Rule function.

     

    Note (general)

    Enter general details for the invoice including notes about the bad debt.

     

    Settlement Status

    Status Due Date

    Enter the date for the settlement status.

     

    Provision in <currency>

    Enter the amount of the provision made in the billing currency.

     

    Exchange

    Provision in <base currency>

    Enter the amount of the provision after conversion to the base currency.

     

    Provision Note

    Enter notes on the provision made.

     

    Update

  • Fee Payable - Click this to find out if there is any fee payable in the selected invoice. The information will be displayed in a separate window. If there is no fee payable, there will not be any information displayed in the Fee Details and Penalty sections of the screen. Refer to Fees Payable.

  • Invoice Penalty - Click this to invoice penalties caused by the late payment of government fees. Refer to Draft Invoices - Creation.

 

For the selected draft invoice, you can select the tabs below to view additional information:

  • Actions - This tab shows the actions for the invoice, if any. You can also add new action. Refer to Action Management.

  • Transactions - This tab shows the receipt/payment transactions related to the invoice.

  • Matters - This tab shows the active Matters of the Billing File.

  • Lines (Details) - The individual detail lines of the selected invoice are displayed here. Double click a line to see the line information, such as the VAT %, exchange rate and discount given.

  • Line Source - This tab is seen only if the selected invoice contains invoice lines from Time or Disbursement entries.

  • Time Entries - This tab is seen if the selected invoice contains invoice line from Time entries. Click this to see Time entries for the invoice including the non-chargeable ones.

  • Disbursement Entries - This tab is seen if the selected invoice contains invoice line from Disbursement entries. Click here to see Disbursement entries for the invoice including the non-chargeable ones.
