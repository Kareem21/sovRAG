Settlements - Multiple Documents and Invoice Lines

If you want to settle multiple invoices or specific invoice lines, then you need to complete the following information when entering a receipt, a payment or when settling to matter.

  1. In the Receipt/Payment screen, do the following:

    • Settle multiple documents - Tick this to list all outstanding invoices for the Billing/Supplier File. If selected together with Settle by Invoice Line, all outstanding invoices and their individual invoice lines will be listed for selection. If you access the Receipt screen from the Settlements screen, then the information that is listed here will be from the same Billing/Supplier File or Revenue Entity depending on the selection criteria you entered.

      When this option is ticked, you can view the header information of an invoice by selecting the invoice and clicking Detail - Header.

    • Settle by invoice line - Tick this to list the invoice lines of the selected invoice. If selected together with Settle Multiple Documents, all outstanding invoices and their individual invoice lines will be listed for selection.

      When this option is ticked, you can view the information for an invoice line by selecting the invoice line and clicking Detail - Line.

    • Load Docs. - Click this to load the invoices and/or the individual invoice lines.

  2. Once the invoices and/or invoice lines have been loaded, you can tick the checkboxes next to the invoices/lines that you want to settle. You can also tick the Select All checkbox to select all invoices/lines.

  3. Enter the amount you want to settle or write off in the Settle or W/O columns respectively. You can see the balance calculation in the summary section on the top right of the screen.

  4. Click Update to save the entry.

Note: You can sort the invoices by clicking on the column headers.
