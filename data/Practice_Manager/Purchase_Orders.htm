Purchase Orders

The Purchase Orders screen is where you can edit, print or post purchase orders. You can access this screen through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Purchase Ledger > Purchase Orders from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Purchase Ledger > Purchase Orders.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Invoicing > Purchase Orders from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Purchase Ledger > Invoicing > Purchase Orders.

You can click on the following to minimise or expand the sections in this screen:

  • - To minimise this section and hide the command buttons. In the grid, only the selected row will be seen.

  • - To expand the section and show the command buttons. If the subtotal line is enabled, it will only be displayed in the expanded view.

Select button

Enter additional search criteria to filter the list. This is seen in Viewpoint - Home.

After you have entered the criteria, click Apply.

 

Filters

You can filter the list of draft invoices by the status of the purchase order - approved or not.

 

Purchase Order list

This shows purchase orders that have been created. You can select a purchase order by clicking on it and this will show the invoice lines in the Lines (Details). To select multiple purchase orders, tick the checkbox for the purchase order.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Edit - Click this to edit the selected purchase order. This will go to the Create/Edit Purchase Orders screen.

  • Select All - Click this to select all records listed in the table.

  • Deselect All - Click this to deselect the selected records.

  • Approve - Click this to approve the purchase order. This needs to be done before you can post a purchase order to an invoice.

  • Undo Approve - Click this to reverse the approval.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected purchase order. If you try to delete an unapproved purchase order (that was previously approved and then unapproved), the system will set the total of the purchase order to zero and not delete it.

  • Print - Click this to print the selected purchase orders. the purchase orders are subsequently locked to prevent new items being added to it. You can reopen them in Create/Edit Purchase Orders screen if you need to do further editing.

    • Document Queue

  • Invoice - Click this to post the selected purchase order. This action is irreversible and the invoices cannot be deleted, but have to be offset with a debit note. If you have not done so, you may be asked to approve the purchase order prior to posting.

    1. In the Invoice Posting window that appears, complete the following information:

      • Invoice Date, Journal Date and Overwrite for (existing) Draft Invoices - When you post an invoice, the invoice and journal dates default to the date when the draft was created. You may overwrite these dates by entering the date you want in the Invoice Date and Journal Date fields, and ticking the corresponding Overwrite for (existing) Draft Invoices checkboxes.

      • Print Document (to document queue) - Tick this checkbox to send a copy of the invoice to the Document Queue for printing or editing.

    2. Click Start to begin the process.

    Note:

    When 'Use Recurring Services for T&D Postings' option is enabled in Home > Configuration > System Defaults > Time and Disbursements, the invoice will be created with the following type:

    • If Entity Administrationcomponent is activated - an invoice with document type 'Recurring Fee Invoice Entity [A_CS]' will be created.
    • If Entity Administration component is not activated -  an invoice with document type 'Recurring Fee Invoice Non-Entity [A_GE]' will be created.

    When 'Use Recurring Services for T&D Postings' option is disabled in Home > Configuration > System Defaults > Time and Disbursements, the invoice will be created with the type 'Debit Note [DR_N]'.

     

     

 

Purchase Order line list

The Purchase Order line list shows the lines of the selected purchase order. If you have selected multiple purchase orders, it shows the lines for the purchase order that are highlighted in blue.

 

Note: If you are using Accounting component and the option 'Generate Journals Automatically' in System Defaults > Accounting, this will create journal entries in the relevant account based on your Account Mapping.
