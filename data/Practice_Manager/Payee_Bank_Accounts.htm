Payee Bank Accounts

This is to create bank accounts of payees for the Supplier File. Records that are created here will be made available for selection for the Remit to field in the Supplier File's General screen. Note that this is only for the Practice Management component. You can also create payee bank accounts through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Sales Ledger Ribbon button and select Sales Ledger > Settings (PM) > Payee/Payer Bank Accounts from the navigation listing in Sales Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Settings (PM) > Payee/Payer Bank Accounts.

 

Filter

You can filter by the status of the payee record or click Select to use other criteria.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to create a new payee.

  • Edit - Click this to edit the details of a payee. If an approved payee is edited, the payee must be re-approved.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected payee.

  • Approval - Click to approve the payee. The approval process depends on how the approval card for payee approval was set up.

    This will open the Approval screen allowing you to review the details and approve the payee by clicking Approve.
