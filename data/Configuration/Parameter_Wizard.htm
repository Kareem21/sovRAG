Parameter Wizard

This is used for configuring e-mail notification for Task Scheduler jobs. You can access Parameter Wizard by clicking on the Browse button next to the field or pressing F12.

 

Note: For e-mail address fields, it is not possible to select a user without an e-mail address from the list.

 

Option list

Double-click to select the option that you want. If you open Parameter Wizard from an e-mail address field, you will see the list of users and their e-mail address. If you open Parameter Wizard from the e-mail subject line or body field, you will see tags to retrieve details of the job.

 

Data entry

Your selection will be seen here. If necessary, you can click in this area to enter additional information by typing.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • OK - Click this to save your selection and input.

  • Close - Click this to cancel your selection and input.
