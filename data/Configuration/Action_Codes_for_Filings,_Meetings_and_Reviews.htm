Action Codes for Filings, Meetings and Reviews

Actions in the Filings, Meetings and Reviews screens is used to keep track of actions for a filing, meeting or review. For each action, it is possible to have the completion of the action do the following:

  • Update record for the filing, meeting or review

  • Trigger change codes

This is done by entering the action codes for the action in the relevant Lookup Table:

  • Filing Actions [FLAC]

  • Meeting Actions [MGAC]

  • Review Actions [RVAC]
