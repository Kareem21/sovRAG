Audit Trail

This screen shows a record of user activities on the Engage Servicing Portal (CP-WP) and is only available in the Front-office environment (CP-AD). When you first access the screen, you will be presented with a selection criteria to filter the records. Enter the criteria for the filter and click Apply to view the audit trail.

The information displayed here cannot be edited. You may redefine the filter criteria by clicking on Select.

 

Note: The maximum number of records displayed in this screen is controlled by 'Maximum Grid Records' field in Configuration > Settings > System Defaults > General/Master File.
