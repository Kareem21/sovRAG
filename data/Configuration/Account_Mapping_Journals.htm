Account Mapping - Journals

The following shows the types of journals that are created when you post from Practice Management to Accounting. The listings on the left are sample data from Practice Management. On the right are the related journals posted through to Accounting for the Revenue Entity and the Client Accounts. If intercompany invoicing is enabled, refer to Intercompany Invoicing and Settlements.

Viewpoint will post the amounts as per option (I). If this is not applicable, then the posting will follow option (II), otherwise (III).

  • I: The INVOICE currency (of the related document) = the BASE currency of the Account File. The posting amounts are taken in the INVOICE currency.

  • II: The BASE currency (of the related document) = the BASE currency of the Account File. The posting amounts are taken in the BASE currency.

  • III: Neither I nor II. The posting amounts are calculated using the INVOICE currency (of the related document) multiplied by the exchange rate on or before the journal date. This is taken from the daily exchange rate tables. The exchange rate for examples below is @ 0.12345, unless specified otherwise.

Note: Purchase Invoices (from the Purchase Ledger) only post to the Revenue Entity.
