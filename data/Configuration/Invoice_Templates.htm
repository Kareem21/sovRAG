Invoice Templates

Invoice templates make your job easier by allowing you to add standard services to a template so that when you issue that invoice, the items are already detailed for you.

 

Invoice template

This shows all the existing invoice templates in the Invoice Templates [IXTP] lookup table.

 

Invoice line

This shows the lines for the selected invoice template.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New  - Click this to add a new line.

    1. Enter details for the invoice line.

    2. Click Update.

  • Edit - Click this to edit an existing line

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected line.
