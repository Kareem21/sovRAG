Service Master - Service Class

Every service added must be assigned to a service class. The available classes are:

  • Ad Valorem Fee [RV] - Based on the Accounting Net Asset Value. Refer to Ad Valorem.

  • Credit: Disbursement charges [CD] - This allows for a Billing File or Matter to have an amount of chargeable disbursements that will not be invoiced. Only when the specified amount has been reached, then subsequent disbursement charges will be invoiced. Refer to Recurring Fee - Disbursement Credit.

  • Credit: Time Charge [CT] - This allows for a Billing File or Matter to have an amount of chargeable time that will not be invoiced. Only when the specified amount has been reached, then subsequent time charges will be invoiced. Refer to Recurring Fee - Time Credit.

  • Disbursement - markup [DM] - Disbursement with markup.

  • Disbursement - normal [DI] - Normal disbursement.

  • Disbursement billed in advance with carry forward [DW] - This allows invoicing the Fixed Fee in advance and later, to invoice in arrears any Disbursement exceeding the Fixed Fee.

  • Expense - non-recoverable [DS] - This is for services that are mainly used in supplier invoices, where the service cost is not recoverable from your clients.

  • Minimum time fee [RN] and [RM] - Use this if you want to charge a minimum amount for a specific service when entering time. Refer to Minimum Time Fee.

  • Professional service [PS].

  • Recurring Fee [RA] - Fee charged at standard intervals.

  • Recurring Fee - excl. for t&d entry [RX] - Similar to [RA] except that this service class will not be available for selection in Time and Disbursements module when the option 'Use recurring services for T&D entries' is ticked in Settings > System Defaults > Practice Management.

  • Time - Administrator Rate [TA] - Time is charged based on the Administrator's rate.

  • Time - Non Chargeable [TN] - Non-chargeable time such as annual leave or public holidays.

  • Time - Service Rate [TC] - Time is charged based on service rates.

  • Time at Fixed Price [TF] - Total amount charged is fixed at the service rate, while the unit price is taken from the Administrator's rate. The difference between the (number of units x administrator rate) and the total amount is written off. In the following example, the service rate is EUR10 whereas the Administrator's rate is EUR15.   • Time billed in advance with carry forward [TW] - This allows invoicing the Fixed Fee in advance and later, to invoice in arrears any Time exceeding the Fixed Fee.

  • Transaction Fee [RT] - Used for automatic calculation of client account transactions and document storing. Refer to Recurring Fee - Transaction Fee.
