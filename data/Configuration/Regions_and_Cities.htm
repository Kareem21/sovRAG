Regions and Cities

When creating new Address Cards with Regions/States or Cities that are not yet in the system, the newly entered Regions/States or Cities can be automatically updated to the respective tables once the new Address Cards are created. This is done by ensuring the 'Automatic Update of City/Region Tables' option is ticked in System Defaults > General/Master File.

If you opt to not automatically update the tables, ensure that the option is not ticked and manually populate the values in the relevant table.

 

Region/State list

The list shows existing regions/states in the Regions table.

 

City list

The list shows existing cities in the Cities table.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add a new region or city.

    • For Region/State - specify the country and enter the new region/state.

    • For City - specify the country and enter the new city. If the city belongs to a region or state, specify that as well.

  • Edit - Click this to edit the selected region or city.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected region or city.

  • Update Globally - Click this to update all address cards whenever you edit a region or city. After performing this, rebuild the Address Card by selecting Full Address Card Descriptions in Configuration > Database Archiving > Troubleshooting.
