Account Mapping - Revenue Entities

The Account Mapping is completed by selecting the Account Type followed by a specific nominal account from the Chart of Accounts (CoA) template in Accounting. This mapping must be completed for each CoA template used in Revenue Entity Account Files.

Receipt/Payments

R.GM

Matter Account Balances

Sales Ledger

  1. When money is received on a Matter account (e.g. client advances cash), the amount will be updated to this account as a Credit entry.

  2. When a settlement is recorded on a sales invoice or money is paid out of a Matter account (e.g. payments out of client cash advances), the amount will be updated to this account as a Debit entry.

Purchase Ledger

  1. When a settlement is recorded on a purchase invoice, a Debit entry matched by corresponding Credit entries (Debits=Credits) will be updated to this account.

1 - Assets Posting

2 - Liability Posting

3 - Expenses Posting

4 - Income Posting

If you are only using the Sales Ledger:

2 - Liability Posting

 

If you are using the Purchase Ledger:

This must be mapped to a clearing account if you are using the Purchase Ledger. The clearing account can be set up as a balance sheet or a profit/loss account.

If set up as a profit/loss account, please ensure that you have cleared it before the financial year-end as any balances will not be carried forward.
