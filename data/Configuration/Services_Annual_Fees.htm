Services (Annual Fees)

Services that are created here are used in the Annual Fees. These services are different from the services created in the Service Master for Practice Management.

 

Filter

The filter applies to the list of service.

 

Service list

This shows all existing service that has been created for Annual Fees.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to create a new service.

    1. Enter details for the service.

    2. Click Update.

  • Edit - Click this to edit existing service.

  • Delete - Click this to remove the selected service.

 

Services (Annual Fees) - Tag

The Note field can be used to enter the [Subscriber...] tag to specify the subscriber(s) for the Service:

  • [SUBSCRIBER=<Subscriber>] is to specify the subscriber group(s) where the service will be replicated to. For multiple groups, use a comma to separate e.g. [SUBSCRIBER=GEN,ASA,EUR].

  • [SUBSCRIBER<><Subscriber>] is to specify subscribers that will be excluded.
