Recalculate/Calendar

The system recalculates account balances after changes have been made to the calendar. Because the enquiry windows follow the calendar, you should roll over to the next financial year at year-end to display the changes in the Trial Balance and Accounts Enquiry folders. Alternatively, you can also generate a statement to view the information.

The Calendar serves as a framework for reporting and enquiry purposes. By default, a monthly calendar of 48 accounting periods is used - this calendar shows two years prior to and two years after the Last Year-End entered in the General screen.

Print Calendar

Validate/Correct

  • Journal Entries - Tick this to validate all journal entries. When this option is ticked, it will do the following:

    • Update all Nominal Account (File) properties (acctType, MainGroup, SubGroup, SpecifySub, AllowSubType, CarryForward, TrustCapInc) to be same as Chart of Accounts (COAaCCount Table).

    • Move invalid journal line to suspense account so that you can correct them before posting. The following can be considered as invalid journal line:

      • Nominal Account is empty.

      • Account does not exist.

      • Invalid journal line - sub-ledger code not specified.

      • Sub-ledger does not exist or Account Code/Type incorrect.

    • Check for any unbalanced journal and create additional line to balance the journal with suspense account.

  •  Settlement Entries - Tick this to validate all settlement entries. This will recalculate the settlement linking.

 

Update & Recalculate

Click this to update the calendar and recalculate the account balances.

 

Cancel

Click to exit the Update Calendar screen without making any changes.

 

OK

This button will replace the Cancel button after you recalculate and update account balances. Click to close the Update Calendar screen.
