Payees - Approval

All payees must be approved before payments can be made to them. The approval process is determined by the approval cards that have been set up and this will determined:

  • the number of approvals required

  • who can perform the approval

When choosing to approve a payee, the Approval screen opens as a separate window.

 

Approval details

This includes the following:

  • Status - this shows the status of the payee record.

  • Payee Details - this shows the Master File for the Payee, Bank, Bank Account, ID/Sort Code and Maximum Amount. For Payee and Bank, you can double-click on the Master File to view its information.

  • Approval Requirements - this summarises the rules for the approval card and approvals made to date for the payee record.

 

Note field

Additional note for the approval can be added to this field.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command buttons to perform the necessary.

  • Approval List - Click this to display the Approval List in the Approval details.

  • Approve/Undo Approval - Click this to approve the payee. Depending on the approval card, the payee may require more than one approval and as long as the required approvals have not been met, you can undo an approval by clicking Undo Approval.

  • Cancel/Close - Click this to exit the Approval screen.
