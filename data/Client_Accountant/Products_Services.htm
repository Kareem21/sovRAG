Using Accounting for Products/Services

This document details how you can use Viewpoint to account for Products and Services in Accounting. This feature makes bookkeeping for trading and reselling-focussed entities more efficient and accurate.

 

Products/Services Sub-ledgers

Create separate sub-ledgers for each stock product. For services, create the sub-ledger with zero stock quantity on hand.

Ensure the following:

  • You have selected the correct Nominal Account where stock product totals will be posted. Note the properties of the nominal account linked to this sub-ledger account:

  • You have ticked the Control Quantity checkbox so that you can enter the stock quantity when creating or editing transactions.

Stock Products Received

When stock products are received, record them at cost. Do this by using a script, or else create the journal manually.

Stock Product Sales: Journal Scripts with Auto-calculating Cost of Sales

Accounting can automatically calculate the Cost of Sales on stock product sales when the sales transactions are recorded. A series of journal scripts have been configured with auto-calculating Cost of Sales on stock product sales. There are three options for calculating Cost of Sales in the journal scripts:

  • Average Cost, which ignores the sub-ledger Valuation setting;

  • First-In-First-Out (FIFO), which also ignores the sub-ledger Valuation setting; and

  • Following the sub-ledger Valuation setting.

These journal scripts are designed so that users only need to enter the Sales component of the transactions, ensuring more accurate and efficient transaction recording of repetitive sales transactions. The Cost of Sales component of the transactions will be automatically added as additional journal lines when the user posts the journal or saves it as a pending journal.

Select the applicable journal script when entering transactions. In the following example, a sales transaction is created in the Debtors sub-ledger.

  1. In the Debtors sub-ledger screen, select the Debit Note - Product Sales journal script.

  2. Click > Script.

  3. Complete the journal details:

 

    • Select Product/ Service line - Select a Product/Service sub-ledger from the list. This will create a sales journal entry in the Income account set up in the properties of the nominal control account linked to the selected sub-ledger.

    • Quantity - Enter the quantity in the same line to auto-calculate the Cost of Sales for this transaction.

  1. Click Post to post the journal or else click Save Pending to save it as a pending journal. If you want to view the entry before posting or saving it as a pending journal, click Journal >.

  2. The journal line entered above will result in the following journal entries:

    • Sales entry - The Income account set up in the properties of the nominal account 2200 - Product Stock, which was linked to the product/service sub-ledger PROD2, is automatically selected. (See Products/Services Sub-ledgers, above.)

 

    • Product issue entry - The Products/Services sub-ledger PROD2 is automatically selected, and 1,000 units issued at cost.

 

    • Cost of Sales entry - The Expenses account set up in the properties of the nominal account 2200 - Product Stock, which was linked to sub-ledger PROD2, is automatically selected, and 1,000 units recorded at cost price.

 

You may raise the above as a manual journal entry rather than using journal scripts.
