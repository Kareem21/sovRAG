Payments Released

The released payments are listed here and by default, filtered by those not yet allocated to a batch. You can access this screen through:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Accounting Ribbon button and select Bank Payment > Payments Released from the navigation listing in Accounting Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Accounting > Bank Payment > Payments Released.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Accounting Ribbon button and select Processing > Payments Released from the navigation listing in Accounting Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Accounting > Processing > Payments Released.

 

Filter

In Viewpoint - Home screen, you can filter the payments according to the category.

 

Payment list

This shows all the payments that have been released.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Print Batch - Click this to print the batch. This is only available in Viewpoint - Home screen.

  • Export/Close - Click this to export and/or close the payment batch. This is only available in Viewpoint - Home screen.

  • Cancel Payment - Click this to cancel the payment.

  • Print Payment - Click this to print the payment.
