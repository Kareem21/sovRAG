Roll Year-End

Account balances are updated after each posting of either a provisional/draft journal or a permanent journal. Therefore, the Trial Balance and Accounts enquiries will always show the latest balances. When the accounting year ends, you can roll over the year-end in order to view the information for next year directly in the enquiry folders.

The enquiry windows follow the calendar, which is regulated by the year-end and period settings. The Posting From/To dates in the General folder will be correspondingly updated by rolling over the year-end.

 

Period setting

Select a period setting for the calendar. This determines how the information in the Trial Balance and Accounts enquiries are grouped.

 

Caption mask

Select the caption mask. This will determine the format of the calendar captions that are displayed when you select a period calendar option, such as in the Trial Balance or Accounts enquiries.

 

Adjust mask by (days)

Enter the number of days to change the year-end. This is necessary if you change the year-end date to one that does not correspond to the end of the month; as such you will need to re-adjust the dates in the Caption column accordingly.

 

Last Year-End

  • one year forward - Selecting this will advance the Last Year-End of the calendar by one year forward.

  • to date: - This allows you to specify a date to roll into for the Last Year-End of the calendar.

 

Print Calendar

Click this to print the calendar.

 

Validate/Correct

  • Journal Entries - Tick this to validate all journal entries. When this option is ticked, it will do the following:

    • Update all Nominal Accounts (File) properties (acctType, MainGroup, SubGroup, SpecifySub, AllowSubType, CarryForward, TrustCapInc) to be same as Chart of Accounts (COAaCCount Table).

    • Move invalid journal line to suspense account so that you can correct them before posting. The following can be considered as invalid journal line:

      • Nominal Account is empty.

      • Account does not exist.

      • Invalid journal line - sub-ledger code not specified.

      • Sub-ledger does not exist or Account Code/Type incorrect.

    • Check for any unbalanced journal and create additional line to balance the journal with suspense account.

  •  Settlement Entries - Tick this to validate all settlement entries. This will recalculate the settlement linking.

 

Update & Recalculate

Click this to update the calendar and recalculate the account balances.

 

Cancel

Click to exit the Change Period-End screen without making any changes.

 

OK

This button will replace the Cancel button after you recalculate and update account balances. Click to close the Change Period-End screen.
