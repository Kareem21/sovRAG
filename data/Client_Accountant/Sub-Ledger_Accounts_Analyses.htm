Sub-Ledger Account - Analyses

This feature shows the details, valuations and movements for the selected sub-ledger account. The information is split into the following:

  • Movements - The account's transactions are summarised by movement type, i.e. opening, closing, debits and credits, in both the sub-ledger currency and the base currency of the Account File. In sub-ledgers where the quantity is controlled, this also includes the quantity movement.

  • Security Valuation - This is only available in Securities sub-ledger accounts, and gives a quick valuation on the security. The balance-to-date is tracked against the initial purchase price. Additionally, a market value is calculated and compared to the book value to determine the gain/loss for the period.

  • Reconciliation - This folder shows the current amount against reconciled and unreconciled amounts.

  • Transactions - This folder shows transactions with journal details.

  • Valuations - This lists saved Account File Valuations, Exchange Revaluations and Sub-ledger Revaluations for the account.

  • Intercompany - This shows intercompany relation between sub-ledgers for the selected sub-ledger or nominal account. If available, it will first show for the selected sub-ledger's currency and then other currencies. By default, transactions in the sub-ledger currency will be shown as well. For transactions in other currencies, users need to expand to view.

  • Security Rebalance - This is only available in Securities sub-ledger accounts and gives a quick valuation on the security. The balance-to-date is tracked against the initial purchase price. Additionally, a market value is calculated and compared to the book value to determine the gain/loss for the period. See also Securities Rebalance

  • Depreciation - This is only available in Fixed Assets sub-ledger accounts and shows the depreciation of the selected sub-ledger within the journal posting window. These depreciation postings can be posted if desired. See also Fixed Asset Depreciation
