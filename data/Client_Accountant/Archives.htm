Archives

When you use the Archive/Change CoA or BC function, the information on the archived accounts is kept here.

 

List of archived accounts

Double-click a record to view details.

 

Command buttons

Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Edit - Click this to add a note to the record.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the record. If you do this, you will lose all the journals related to the archive.

 

How do I view archived journals?

  1. In File Maintenance screen, click the Accounts Ribbon button.

  2. Click Account.

  3. In Account Enquiry screen, under the Account tab, click the Browse button for Select Ledger.

  4. Click the record you want under Archive.

  5. Click OK.
