Bank Payments

This feature is an additional option and is only available if included in your licence.

 

The Bank Payments feature in the Accounting component enables you to create and approve payees/bank payments; as well as subsequently prepare a bank payment export file to be sent to your bank(s) for further processing. It comprises:

  • Approval Cards - Define approval cards containing the rules on the user(s) and/or group(s) of users authorised to approve payees and payments; and the amount they are authorised to approve.

  • Bank Export Batch configuration - Bank Master Files can be configured to create export batch files, which will be sent to banks for further processing. This is through the Payment Types & Export Batches screen.

  • Payee setup and Approval - Set up payees with bank payment details and a maximum authorised payment amount. Payees will be approved based on the rules in the approval cards. This can be done through:

    • Payees and Payments & Approvals screens for Accounting

    • Distributions and Assets screens for Entity Administration

    • Payee Bank Accounts, Payer Bank Accounts and Payee/Payer Bank Accounts screens for Practice Management

  • Payment creation and Approval - Create payments for pre-approved payees in Client Accountant, Entity Administration and Practice Management. Payments will be approved based on the rules in the approval cards.

  • Payment Release and Batch (Re)Assign - Approved payments are released to a manual or an exportable bank payment batch. Once released, the payment will automatically create a pending journal using the sub-ledger details and optionally, the journal script selected during payment creation. These payments can be reassigned to a different payment batch as needed. This can be done in the Payments Released screen.

  • Export and/or Close Payment Batches - Once the payment batches are complete, prepare the export batch files (where applicable) for subsequent transmission to the bank and close the payment batches. This is through the Payment Types & Export Batches screen.

To use this feature, ensure the following:

  • Set up the approval cards in Approval Cards screen - after this has been set up, then you can create payees with bank payment details and a maximum authorised payment amount. Payees will be approved base on the rules in payee approval cards.

  • Verify/configure journal scripts - it is recommended that Bank Account sub-ledge journal script(s) for Bank Payments have been verified and properly configured as these journal scripts are used when creating new pending journals upon the release of an approved payment to a bank payment batch.

  • Obtain export file configuration from your bank - if you wish to send export batch files to your bank for further processing, you need to have the file requirements from the bank before you start the configuration of the export file. Currently, the exportable file formats are csv and xml.
