Payment - New

Payments can only be created for approved payees. Similar to payee records, payments can be created in both Accounting and Entity Administration components.

 

Accounting Component

New payment record can be set up in:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Accounting Ribbon button and select Bank Payment > Pending Payments from the navigation listing in Accounting Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Accounting > Bank Payment > Pending Payments.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Accounting Ribbon button and select Processing > Pending Payments from the navigation listing in Accounting Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Accounting > Processing > Pending Payments.

In the screen, click New to enter information for the payment and click Update.

 

Entity Administration Component

In Entity Administration, the payment record can be created in the following screens by clicking Payment (CA) after the payee has been approved:

  • Distributions

  • Bank Accounts (under Assets/Liabilities)

  • Assets (under Assets/Liabilities)

  • Open Items (under Assets/Liabilities)

  • Securities (under Assets/Liabilities)

In the screen, enter information for the payment and click Update.
