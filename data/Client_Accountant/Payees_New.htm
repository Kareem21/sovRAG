Payees - New

Payee records can be created in Accounting and Entity Administration components but the approvals can only be done in Accounting within the Payees & Approval screen.

 

Accounting Component

New payee record can be set up in:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Accounting Ribbon button and select Bank Payment > Payees & Approvals from the navigation listing in Accounting Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Accounting > Bank Payment > Payees & Approvals.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Accounting Ribbon button and select Processing > Payees from the navigation listing in Accounting Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Accounting > Processing > Payees.

In the screen, click New to enter information for the payee and click Update.

 

Entity Administration Component

New payee record can be set up in the following screens:

  • Distributions

  • Bank Accounts (under Assets/Liabilities)

  • Assets (under Assets/Liabilities)

  • Open Items (under Assets/Liabilities)

  • Securities (under Assets/Liabilities)

In the screen, click Payment (CA) to enter information for the payee and click Update.

Note: If there is an existing record for the payee, clicking Payment (CA) will allow you to create Payment record instead.

 

 

Practice Management Component

For Practice Management, the records can be set up in the following screens:

  • Viewpoint - Home screen:

    • Click Sales Ledger Ribbon button and select Settings (PM) > Payee/Payer Bank Accounts from the navigation listing in Sales Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click Home > Practice Management > Settings (PM) > Payee/Payer Bank Accounts.

  • File Maintenance screen:

    • Click Sales Ledger Ribbon button and select Invoicing > Payer Bank Accounts from the navigation listing in Sales Ledger Overview.

    • Click Purchase Ledger Ribbon button and select Invoicing > Payee Bank Accounts from the navigation listing in Purchase Ledger Overview.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Sales Ledger > Invoicing > Payer Bank Accounts.

    • In Section Menu, click File > Purchase Ledger > Invoicing > Payee Bank Accounts.

In the screen, click New to enter information and click Update.
