Connect - Password Activation

This job uses the Connect Login Activation E-mail [VPACTPWD] template to send out links to new users to activate their passwords.

 

Setting up Task Scheduler

  1. Go to Configuration > Business Parameters > Task Scheduler > Agent Job Scheduler.

  2. Click New

  3. Select the Thread Number.

  4. Ensure that the Job Type is set to 'Connect - Password Activation [VPAP]'.

  5. Click Next >.

  6. In the Criteria tab, the E-mail Subject and E-mail Template fields will show the following default values based on the job type Connect - Password Activation [VPAP]:

    • E-mail Subject - Viewpoint Password Assistance

    • E-mail Template - Connect Login Activation E-mail

  7. Click Next >.

  8. If an e-mail notification is required, tick Enable E-mail Notification and specify details for the e-mail.

  9. Click Next.

  10. In Schedule, the Job Frequency to Continuous [C].

  11. Click Done.

  12. Click Yes to confirm job creation.
