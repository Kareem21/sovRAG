AdobeSign - Download and Check In Signed Documents

The task scheduler job 'AdobeSign - Download and Check In Signed Documents [ESAS]' downloads and checks in signed documents from Adobe Sign into Viewpoint, hence users do not need to perform the document check in manually.

 

Setting up Task Scheduler

  1. Go to Configuration > Business Parameters > Task Scheduler > Agent Job Scheduler.

  2. Click New and ensure that the Job Type is set to 'AdobeSign - Download and Check-in Signed Documents [ESAS]'.

  3. Click Next >.

  4. In the ‘Criteria’ tab, enter the Adobe Access Token value. This value is created in the Adobe Sign account.

  5. Click Next >.

  6. If an e-mail notification is required, tick Enable E-mail Notification and specify details for the e-mail.

  7. Click Next >.

  8. In Schedule, enter the job frequency.

  9. Click Done.
