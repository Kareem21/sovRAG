Document Virus Scan

The Document Virus Scan job scans documents in Viewpoint Connect database for virus. Based on the virus definition of a third-party component, any corrupted documents found will be moved to the Quarantined Documents screen where the documents can be stored to a location in the hard drive, marked as safe, or permanently deleted.

 

Setting up Task Scheduler

  1. Go to Configuration > Business Parameters > Task Scheduler > Agent Job Scheduler.

  2. Click New and ensure that the Job Type is set to 'Document Virus Scan [VRSC]'.

  3. Click Next >.

  4. In the Criteria tab, enter the value and select the option for the following mandatory fields:

    • Total Document - Enter the number of documents you want this job to scan.

    • Scan Order - The options available are:

      • Oldest to Newest – Scan documents starting from oldest to newest added.

      • Newest to Oldest – Scan documents starting from newest to oldest added.

  5. Click Next >.

  6. If an e-mail notification is required, tick Enable E-mail Notification and specify details for the e-mail.

  7. Click Next >.

  8. In Schedule, enter the job frequency.

  9. Click Done.

Note: Scanning is done once per document. Subsequent scanning will only be performed on unscanned documents.
