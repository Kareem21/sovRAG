Notification - Delete Read/Unflagged Notifications

This job type will remove notifications items from the Notifications screen based on the age of the items as long as the notification item has been read and not flagged for any follow-up.

 

Setting up Task Scheduler

  1. Go to Configuration > Business Parameters > Task Scheduler > Agent Job Scheduler.

  2. Click New and ensure that the Job Type is set to 'Notification - Delete Read/Unflagged Notifications [DNOT]'.

  3. Click Next >.

  4. In Criteria, specify the value for 'Message older than'.

  5. Click Next >.

  6. If an e-mail notification is required, tick 'Enable E-mail Notification' and specify details for the e-mail.

  7. Click Next >.

  8. In Schedule, enter the job frequency.

  9. Click Done.
