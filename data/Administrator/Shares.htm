Shares

The Shares folder is split into four sections and each section displays different information with different functions. This folder is used to record share transactions and displays information on the entity's authorised share capital and share members.

 

Where do I record a share transaction?

Depending on the type of share transaction, you will either use the Capital or Members section.

If necessary, you can reverse share transactions in the Transactions section.

How do I post a share transaction?

Go to Posting section and select the share transaction you want to post. Click Post.

Where do I assign a Beneficial Owner?

You can set up beneficial owner in the Posting or Members section.

See also Tips: Beneficial Owner

 

Posting

This section lists incomplete share transactions which are transactions without effective date. Find out more here.

 

Capital

This section displays a complete record of share capital information for all share classes authorised for the entity. Find out more here.

 

Shareholders

This section is where shares are transferred, beneficial owners assigned, calls paid; and certificates redeemed, cancelled or forfeited. Find out more here.

 

Transaction

This section lists all transactions for all share classes. Find out more here.
