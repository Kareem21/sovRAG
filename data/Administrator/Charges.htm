Charges

The charges for an entity are maintained here.

 

Filter

You can filter the charges by its status.

Active - Click to view only active charges.

Inactive - Click to view charges that have discharged.

All - Click to view all charges.

 

Charges list

This shows the charges for the Entity File.

 

Command buttons

Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add a new charge.

    Enter the details for the charge. Click Update when done.

  • Edit - Click this to edit the details of the selected charge record.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Discharge - Click this to enter the discharge date to cancel the charge.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected charge record.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.
