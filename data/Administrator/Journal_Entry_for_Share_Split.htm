Journal Entry for Share Split

For share split transaction, when entering the journal entry, do the following:

  1. Set the Movement type as Acquisition [EA].

  2. Enter the additional quantity.

  3. Enter Nil for the Amount.

  1. Click Update.

  2. Click Ignore for the prompt message.

  3. Click Post.

  4. Confirm posting the journal.

  5. Click Ignore for the second prompt message.
