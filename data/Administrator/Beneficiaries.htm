Beneficiaries

Details of a trust's beneficiaries are recorded in this folder and each beneficiary must have a Master file set up.

 

Filter

You can filter the beneficiary list by selecting from the Status dropdown box.

  • Current - Displays the records where the 'Revoked Date' is in the future and/or without 'Revoked Date'.

  • Past - Displays the records where the 'Revoked Date' has already passed.

  • All - Displays all Current and Past records.

 

Beneficiary list

This shows the beneficiaries of the entity.

 

Command buttons

Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to appoint a beneficiary.

    Enter the details for the beneficiary. Click Update when done.

  • Edit - Click this to edit the selected beneficiary's details.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the beneficiary's record.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

  • Bank Details - Click this to add bank account for the beneficiary.

    1. Enter the details of the beneficiary's bank account.

    2. Click Update when done.
