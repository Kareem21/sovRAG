Registers - Company/Trust

The Registers screen contains command buttons that you can click to generate documents and update date for certain events and print registers.

For managed entities, the events are incorporation, Annual General Meeting, Extraordinary General Meeting, Annual Return, audits and filing of accounts. For trusts, the events are setting up of trust, audits and filling of accounts.  

 

Registers - Company

Command buttons

Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Incorporation - Click this to incorporate the company and generate the relevant forms and minutes. The incorporation details are based on those entered in Statutory folder.

    1. Click Yes to continue.

    2. Tick the documents you want to generate for the incorporation.

    3. Click OK.

  • Notice AGM - Click this to generate AGM notice.

  • AGM - Click this to generate documents for Annual General Meeting like the meeting minutes. The dates must be entered in M&A/Dates. This will also update these:

    1. Click Yes to continue.

    2. Tick the document(s) you want to generate.

    3. Click OK.

    4. Click Yes to update the AGM dates.

     

    • Last Annual Meeting - Date of last AGM.

    • Next Annual Meeting - Date for next AGM.

  • Annual Return - Click this to generate the Annual Return for the entity. The dates must be entered in M&A/Dates. This will also update these:

    1. Click Yes to continue.

    2. Tick the document(s) you want to generate.

    3. Click OK.

    4. Click Yes to update the dates for Annual Return.

    • Last Annual Return - Date of last Annual Return.

    • Next Annual Return - Date for next Annual Return.

  • EGM - Click this to generate documents for Extraordinary General Meeting.

    1. Click EGM.

    2. Click Yes to continue.

    3. Tick the document(s) you want to generate.

    4. Click OK.

    5. Click OK once more.

  • Complete Audit - Click this to generate documents related audit of accounts. This will also update:

    1. Click Yes to continue.

    2. Tick the form(s) you want to generate.

    3. Click OK.

    4. Click Yes to update the date.

     

    • Date Last Audit - Date for the last account audit in Accounts.

  • File Accounts - Click this to generate documents related to filing of accounts. This will also update:

    1. Click Yes to continue.

    2. Tick the form(s) you want to generate.

    3. Click OK.

    4. Click Yes to update the date.

     

    • Date Accounts Files - Date of when accounts was last filed in Accounts.

  • Print Registers - Click this to print company information sheets and statutory registers.

    Can I change the template used?

    You can set the templates to be used for each entity type in Configuration > Business Parameters > Administrator > Entity Types. For the selected entity type, enter the following in Tag Values:

    [WREP.DefaultFormCode=NewFormCode]

     

    See also:

    • Entity Type - Tags

    This function allows you to choose and print documents for the entity.

    1. Tick the document(s) you want to print.

    2. Click Generate.

    3. Select the output method for the document(s).
