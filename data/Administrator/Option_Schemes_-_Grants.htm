Grants

This section is where you maintain the grants for the selected option scheme. There are two sections in this screen and you can click on:

  • - To minimise this section and hide the command buttons. In the grid, only the selected row will be seen.

  • - To expand the section and show the command buttons.

 

Filters

 Use the filter to select the scheme and the grant status e.g. Active or Inactive.

 

Grant list

This lists the different grants that have been issued for the selected option scheme.

 

Filter

Use the filter to select the scheme and the grant status e.g. Active or Inactive.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to create a new grant.

    Enter the details of the grant. Click Update when done.

  • Edit - Click this to edit the details of an existing grant.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected grant. This will also delete all related certificates and transactions.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

  • Deactivate/Reactivate - Click this to deactivate or re-activate the selected grant.

  • Adjust Global - Click to adjust the exercise price of all certificates issued for the selected grant.

    Enter the details of adjustment. Click Update when done.

 

Transactions

This lists the transactions for the selected grant.

 

Command buttons

These apply to the transactions. Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Confirm - Click this to enter the effective date for an outstanding transaction. If you have selected a confirmed transaction, the button will be disabled. You can also confirm outstanding transaction in Transactions section.

  • Edit Note - Click this to edit the note related to the transaction.

  • Undo - Click this to undo the transaction. Transactions can only be undone from the most recent event backwards.
