Transactions

This section displays all the transactions for a particular option scheme. You can use filter the transactions by scheme, the transaction type and grant or member.

 

Confirm

Click to enter the effective date and confirm the transaction.

 

Payment

Click to update the payment amount received. This is similar to clicking Payment in Certificates section.

Enter the payment details. Click Update when done.

 

Edit Note

Click to edit the note related to the transaction.

 

Undo

Click to undo the transaction.

 

Share Cert.

Click to assign a transaction or certificate number. You do this to execute an option so you can use the filter to list only Exercised and not Executed options.

Enter the details of the execution. Click Update when done.
