Global Change

Global changes are performed across the entire database. A global change can be made for replacing/resigning officers, changing a registered office and changing signatories on bank accounts. This means you enter the information once and the change will be updated in every applicable record. In addition, Administrator will generate all the necessary forms and minutes that relate to the changes you make in the Global change folder.

You can globally change the following:

  • Relation/Officer - To globally replace/resign the person/corporation.

  • Address Card - Master File - To globally change a Master File address.

  • Registered Office Address - Entity - To globally change the registered office address of an entity.

  • Business Registration Address - To globally change a business registration address.

  • Bank Signatory - To globally change signatory details.

For all Global Change functions, you will see:

  • Settings folder - In this folder, specify the Master File/Address Card and conditions for the global change.

  • Selection folder - In this folder, select the Master Files to which you want to apply the global change by ticking the record; or

    • Select All - Click this to select all records.

    • Deselect All - Click this to deselect all records.

    • Start - Click this to start the global change process.
