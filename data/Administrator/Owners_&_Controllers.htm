Owners & Controllers

This screen records the beneficial owners and controllers for filings and registers. There are two sections in this screen and you can click on:

  • - To minimise this section and hide the command buttons. In the grid, only the selected row will be seen.

  • - To expand the section and show the command buttons.

 

Filters

Click to filter by the status of the type.

 

Owners & Controllers list

This shows records of owner or controller that have been added.

 

Command buttons

These apply to the list of owners and controllers. Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add new record for an owner or controller.

    1. Enter details for the new Owners & Controllers:

      • Register Jurisdiction - Select from the list which includes only the incorporation country for the entity and countries where the entity has a foreign registration.

      • Owner/Controller - Select the Master File. Leave it blank if the Owner or Controller is unidentified or non-registrable.

      • Direct Owner - Select the direct owner of the Master File.

      • Date Commenced - Enter the commencement date for the Owner or Controller.

      • Date Ceased - Enter the ceased date of the Owner or Controller.

      • Date in Register - Enter the registration date for Owner or Controller.

      • Date out of Register - Enter the date of Owner or Controller being removed from the Register.

      • Percentage Interest - Enter the percentage held.

      • Percentage Vote - Enter the percentage of votes held.

      • Note - Enter any additional notes if any.

      Note: Depending on the jurisdiction, the available fields may differ from the list above.

      2. Click Update.

       

     

  • Edit - Click this to edit selected record.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected record.

 

Owners & Controllers Action list

This shows actions of the selected owner or controller.

 

Command buttons

These apply to the list of actions. Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add a new action.

  • Edit - Click this to edit the selected action.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected action.

  • Complete - Click this to complete the selected action.
