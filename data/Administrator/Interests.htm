Interests

Interest held by beneficial owners or ultimate owners is recorded here, and is separate and distinct from the beneficial owner information captured in the Shares folder. You need to decide which folder you will be using before you enter any information.

The Interests folder is available for all non-managed entities (except Individual Master Files) and allows you to enter, review and maintain beneficial or ultimate ownership details.

 

Filters

Click to filter by the status and ownership type.

You can select Beneficial Owners, Ultimate Ownership, Others or All.

 

Interest records

This shows interest records that have been added. If the Master File of the interest holder is shown as [Hidden], this means that you do not have access to ownership information.

This is an additional authority setting assigned to User Group(s). Refer to User Group - Authorities.

 

Command buttons

Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add new record for beneficial owner or pledge/ultimate owner/holding company. Additional interest types can be set up in Interest Types [INTP] lookup table.

    Enter details for the owner/holding company. Click Update when done.

    Note: The fields here are standard fields for Pledge, Ultimate Holding Company and Ultimate Owner Interest Types. You may see different fields in your environment. Refer to Interest Types [INTP] lookup table for more information.

     

    Enter details for the beneficial owner. Click Update when done.

    Note: The fields here are standard fields for Beneficial Owner Interest Type. You may see different fields in your environment. Refer to Interest Types [INTP] lookup table for more information.

  • Edit - Click this to edit selected record.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected record.

  • Move Up - Click this to move the selected record up.

  • Move Down - Click this to move the selected record down.
