Dividends

The Dividends folder allows you to keep track of dividends declared and paid.

 

Dividends list

This shows the dividends of the entity.

 

Command buttons

Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to enter a dividend record.

    Enter the dividend details. Click Update when done.

  • Edit - Click this to edit selected dividend record.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected dividend record.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.
