Bank/Broker Accounts & Signatories

The Bank/Broker Accounts & Signatories folder is used to store information on bank/broker accounts and the bank/broker signatories. These are kept in two different tables and you can click on:

  • - To minimise this section and hide the command buttons. In the grid, only the selected row will be seen.

  • - To expand the section and show the command buttons.

 

Filter

You can filter the list of bank/broker account by the bank/broker account status.

Open - Click to view only open bank accounts.

Closed - Click to view bank accounts that have been closed.

All - Click to view all bank accounts.

 

Bank/Broker Accounts list

This lists all bank/broker accounts for the entity. These include bank/broker accounts that have been closed.

 

Command buttons

These apply to the bank/broker accounts list. Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add a new bank/broker account.

    1. Enter the bank account details.

    2. Click Update when done..

  • Edit - Click this to edit details of the selected bank/broker account record.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Close - Click this to close the selected bank/broker account. The bank/broker account record will still remain in the table but no further transaction is possible for the bank/broker account.

    1. Enter the Date Closed and Note (if any).

    2. Click Update.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected bank/broker account record. This will also delete the signatories for the account.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

 

Can I close a bank/broker account without entering the date closed?

Yes, you can leave the field blank and enter it at a later time.

When do I delete a bank/broker account?

If the bank/broker account was erroneously created, it is correct to delete it. However, if you are closing a bank/broker account, then do not delete the bank/broker account.

 

Can I link the record entered to a stored document?

Yes, you can. A record link can be created to link the record to document holders. This can be done either using right-click option or through Tool - Linked Documents.

Filter

You can filter the list of bank/broker account by the status.

Current - Click to view active signatories.

Past - Click to view signatories that have been cancelled.

All - Click to view all signatories.

 

Signatories list

This lists the signatories for the selected bank/broker account. You can filter the list by signatory status.

Current - Click to view active signatories.

Past - Click to view signatories that have been cancelled.

All - Click to view all signatories.

 

Command buttons

These apply to the signatories list. Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add signatory for the selected bank/broker account.

    Enter the signatory details. Click Update when done.

  • Edit - Click this to edit details of the selected signatory.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Cancel - Click this to cancel the signatory. This will flag the signatory as inactive.

    1. Enter the Date Cancelled.

    2. Click Update.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected signatory.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

  • Copy Signatories - Click this to copy signatories from one bank/broker account to another. This is enabled when you have at least one bank/broker account with its signatories set up. When you add a new bank/broker account without any signatories, you can then copy the signatories from the existing bank/broker account to the new bank/broker account.

    1. Select the bank account from which you want to copy the signatories from.

    2. Enter the resolution date.

    3. Click Update.

 

Can I cancel the signatory without entering the date cancelled?

Yes, you can leave the field blank and enter it at a later time.
