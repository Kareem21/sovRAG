Posting

Incomplete partnership transactions are transactions without an effective date and these will be seen here. The system will treat them as outstanding items and the transactions will be listed in the To-Do List to remind you of these transactions.

 

Transactions

This lists all transactions without any effective date.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Post - Click this to post the selected transaction.

    1. Enter the effective date.

    2. Enter note, if applicable.

    3. Click Update.

  • Delete - Click this to delete an unconfirmed transaction.

    To prevent accidental deletion, you will need to click Yes to confirm the deletion once you have selected the transaction and clicked Delete.

    If you have deleted a share authorisation, go to Transactions section and click Rebuild to rebuild the share register.

    Also, you are not allowed to delete a transaction that precedes another transaction. For example, if you authorise 100 shares and allot 20 shares, you cannot delete the authorisation without first deleting the allotment.

     

What is the consequence of not posting a partnership transaction?

The transactions will not be reflected in the partnership register.
