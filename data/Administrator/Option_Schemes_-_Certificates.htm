Certificates

This section is for you to maintain the certificates for a selected grant. The information is displayed in two tables and you can click on:

  • - To minimise this section and hide the command buttons. In the grid, only the selected row will be seen.

  • - To expand the section and show the command buttons.

 

Filter

Use the filter to select the scheme you want to work with, the option type and the grant or member.

Specify if you want to filter by grant or member and then select the Master File for the grant or member.

Select whether you want to see Active, Waiting for Acceptance, Accepted and not Lapsed, Accepted and Lapsed, Inactive or All Options.

 

Certificate description list

This lists the different certificates that have been issued for the selected grant.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to create a new certificate.

    Enter the details of the certificate. Click Update when done.

  • Accept - Click this to accept the option. An option holder must accept the offer before exercising it. The offer will lapse if it is not exercised by the cut-off date.

    Enter details of the acceptance. Click Update when done.

  • Lapse - Click this to lapse the option. This is enabled only for certificates that have been accepted and paid for.

    Enter the details for the option lapse. Click Update when done.

  • Exercise - Click this to exercise the option. This is enabled only for certificates that have been accepted and paid for.

    Enter the details of the exercise. Click Update when done.

  • Edit - Click to edit the details of an existing certificate.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click to delete the selected certificate. This will also remove all related transaction history.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

  • Adjust Exercise Price - Click to adjust the exercise price. This allows you to adjust the exercise price for the selected certificate.

    1. Enter the details of t he adjustment:

      • Transaction Date - This defaults to today's date but you can change it.

      • Effective Date - Enter the effective date of the transaction.

      • Adjust Quantity Factor - Enter the adjustment factor for the number of options granted.

      • Adjusted No. Granted - The number of options granted based on the adjustment factor is displayed here. You can also enter the number of options to be granted and the system will calculate the Adjust Quantity Factor.

      • Adjust Price Factor - Enter the price adjustment factor for the options granted.

      • Adjusted Exercise Price - The exercise price based on the adjustment factor is displayed here. You can also enter the exercise price you want and the system will calculate the Adjust Price Factor.

      • Note - Enter notes for the adjustment.

    2. Click Update.

Can I adjust the exercise price for all certificates issued for a grant?

Yes, you can do this by clicking Adjust Global in Grants section.

 

Transactions

This lists all transactions for the selected certificate.

 

Command buttons

These apply to the transactions. Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Confirm - Click this to enter the effective date for an outstanding transaction. If you have selected a confirmed transaction, the button will be disabled. You can also confirm outstanding transaction in Transactions section.

  • Payment - Click this to enter payment for accepted options. This is similar to clicking Payment in Transaction section.

    Enter the payment details. Click Update when done.

  • Edit Note - Click this to edit the note for the transaction.

  • Undo - Click this to undo the transaction.  Transactions can only be undone from the most recent event backwards.
