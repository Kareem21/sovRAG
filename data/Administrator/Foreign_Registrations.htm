Foreign Registrations

This folder is used to record the Entity File's foreign registrations and generate the foreign entity's annual returns and accounts.

 

Filter

You can filter the registrations by its status.

Active - Click to view only active registrations.

Inactive - Click to view registrations that have ceased.

All - Click to view all registrations.

 

Registrations list

This shows the registrations for the Entity File.

 

Command buttons

Click the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New - Click this to add foreign registration.

    Enter the registration information. Click Update when done.

  • Edit - Click this to edit details of the foreign registration.

    Edit the necessary details. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected registration record.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

  • Cessation - Click this to cease the registration. The record will remain on the business list until it is deleted.

    1. Enter the Cessation Date.

    2. Enter any relevant note.

    3. Click Update.

  • Annual Return - Click this to generate the annual return for the selected foreign registration. This is possible when you have entered the annual return date next.

    1. Click Yes to continue with the annual return generation.

    2. Click Yes to update the annual return dates.

     

  • Accounts - Click this to update the last filed date for the accounts of the selected foreign registration. This is possible when you have entered the accounts' due date.

    1. Click Yes to confirm the date and continue.

    2. Click Yes to update the date.

 

Why am I not able to generate the annual return?

If you have not entered the annual return date next for the registration, you will see a prompt informing you of this. Click OK and edit the registration record to enter the date.

Can I have more than one foreign registration per jurisdiction?

This is possible if you enable the option in Configuration > Settings > System Defaults > Entity/Forms.
