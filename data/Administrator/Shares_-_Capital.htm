Capital

In this section, you can authorise and allot shares, edit a share class and perform capital change. There are two tables in this section:

  • Share Classes

    This table contains the share classes for the company and for each share class, you will see information such as the name and currency. Depending on the ownership structure, you may also see the following:

    • Nominal Value - this is the nominal value as specified when creating the share class

    • Divided Into - this is the number of shares as specified when creating the share class

    • Issued - this is the total of issued shares

    • Available - this is the number of shares that have not been issued

    • Authorised Capital - this is the number of shares specified when creating the share class multiplied by the nominal value

    • Paid-up Capital - this is the total of payments received for issued shares

    • Stated Capital - this is calculated by multiplying the number of shares issued with the nominal value per share

    Note: If a value equals or less than 0 in entered is the 'Number of Shares' and 'Nominal Value' fields for 'Authorised Capital [SA]' ownership structure, a validation message will be prompted.

  • Authorisations and Allotments

    Lists all authorisations and allotments for selected share class in the Authorised Share Capital table. The table shows the transaction date, the effective date (posted date) and description of the transaction.

    For example: Allotment of 100 ordinary shares for USD100 to Viewpoint Ltd.

    How can I customise the description?

    Customise description for share authorisations in the Share:Authorization Types [S_AU] lookup value table.

    Customise description for share allotments in the Share:Allotment/IssueTypes [S_AL] lookup value table.

For each section, you can click on:

  • - To minimise this section and hide the command buttons. In the grid, only the selected row will be seen.

  • - To expand the section and show the command buttons.

 

What is a capital increase?

A capital increase is when the directors decide to increase the number of shares in a share class for the entity. This may occur when all of the entity's authorised capital has been issued and paid up.

The decision to increase the authorised capital may be a result of the entity's business expanding.

 

What is a capital decrease?

A capital decrease is when the directors decide to decrease the number of shares in a share class. This may be due to the entity's business not performing as well as it could.

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • New Class - Click to create/authorise a new share class.

    1. Enter the relevant information.

    2. Click Update.

    3. Tick the form(s) you want to generate relating to this authorisation.

    4. Click OK.

    Note: If the option 'Use Distinctive Numbers' is ticked, the distinctive number will be system-generated and the Distinctive Numbers field will not be editable when issuing or transferring shares. The Distinctive Number will start at 1 for each share class.

  • Increase/Decrease - Click to increase or decrease the authorised capital of the selected share class.

    1. Enter the relevant information for the capital decrease.

    2. Click Update.

    3. Tick the form(s) you want to generate relating to this authorisation.

    4. Click OK.

    1. Enter the relevant information for the capital increase.

    2. Click Update.

    3. Tick the form(s) you want to generate relating to this authorisation.

    4. Click OK.

  • Edit Class - Click to edit the selected share class.

    1. Edit the necessary details.

    2. Click Update after you have completed the editing.

    If you tick the option 'Freeze Share Class', this will disallow further share transactions on the selected share class as the following command buttons in these screens will be disabled:

    • Capital

      • Increase/Decrease

      • Capital Change

      • Issue

      • User Fields

    • Posting

      • Post

      • Delete

      • Ben. Owner

    • Shareholders

      • Transfer

      • Edit Cert.

      • Ben. Owner

      • Call(s) Paid

      • Redeem

      • Cancel Cert.

      • Forfeit

    • Transactions

      • Reverse

      • Edit

      • Explore

  • Capital Change - Click to perform capital change on the selected share class. A capital change occurs when there is a change in the authorised capital of an entity. This may be reflected by creating a new share class, redeeming existing shares or allotting new shares. 

  • Issue - Click to issue shares for the selected share class.

    1. Enter the information for the issuance.

    2. Click Update.

    3. Tick the form(s) you want to generate.

    4. Click OK.

  • Explore - Click this to open the Share Structure Explorer screen. This button is turned off by default.

  • User Fields - This section allows you to retrieve additional fields to record specific information on an entity's shares.
