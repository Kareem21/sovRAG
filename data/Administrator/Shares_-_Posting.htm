Posting

All share transaction require an effective date in order for them to be added to the share capital and members register. Without a confirmed date, the transactions will be treated as unposted and reminders will be created in the Reminders. These transactions will not be included in the share capital or members register until they are posted. For share authorisations that have not been posted, you may still allot the shares.

 

Transactions

This lists all share transactions without any effective date.

 

Command buttons

Click on the relevant command button to perform the necessary.

  • Post - Click this to post share transaction.

    1. Enter the effective date.

    2. Enter note, if applicable.

    3. Click Update.

  • Delete - Click this to delete an unconfirmed share transaction.

    To prevent accidental deletion, you will need to click Yes to confirm the deletion once you have selected the transaction and clicked Delete.

    If you have deleted a share authorisation, go to Transactions section and click Rebuild to rebuild the share register.

    Also, you are not allowed to delete a transaction that precedes another transaction. For example, if you authorise 100 shares and allot 20 shares, you cannot delete the authorisation without first deleting the allotment.

     

  • Ben. Owner - Click this to set up beneficial owner for allotments that have not been posted.

    1. Select the Master File for the beneficial owner.

    2. Click Update.

    See also Tips:Beneficial Owner

Why am I not able to see the Ben. Owner button?

This command button may be turned off to have the beneficial owner recorded in Interests screen..

To check, go to Configuration > Settings > Customise > Field Settings and specify the following:

  • Menu Group = File

  • Section = Entity Administration

  • Screen = Shareholders
