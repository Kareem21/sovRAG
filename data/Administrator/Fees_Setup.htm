Fees Setup

This is only enabled for data entry if the Maintain Itemised Annual Fees check box is ticked in the File Setup folder. The total annual fee is calculated automatically and displayed at the bottom of the table.

 

Annual Fees List

This lists the annual fee items for the entity.

 

Command buttons

  • New - Click this to add a new Annual Fee item.

    Enter the details. Click Update when done.

     

  • Edit - Click this to edit the selected item.

  • Delete - Click this to delete the selected item.

    Click Yes to confirm the deletion.

 

Total Fee

This shows the total fee of all Annual Fee items.
